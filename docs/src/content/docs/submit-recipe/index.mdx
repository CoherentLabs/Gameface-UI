Submit via a GitHub issue - attach your recipe files there (**required**). Title, description, and usage steps are **recommended**.

:::note[Requirements]
- **Required:** Recipe files on the issue (not reviewed without files).
- **Recommended:** Title, description, how-to-use.
- **Optional:** Tags and your name.

**A GitHub account is required.**
:::

## Terms of contribution

By submitting a recipe, you confirm that:

- You have the right to share the recipe and any included assets.
- You agree that accepted recipes may be published in the Gameface UI docs under the project’s open-source license.
- Your submission may be reviewed, edited, or rejected by the maintainers.
